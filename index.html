SECRET SANTA

We are getting ready to play a game of Secret Santa !

Here are the guidelines :

1. You will get a chit with one of the team member's name - you will be the Secret Santa for that person.

2. Every day, please make it fun by sending an anonymous note to that person. The note can highlight something funny about that person's statements, some funny anecdotes about him/her, some tasks, or something totally unrelated - like a joke from the internet, a comic strip like Dilbert or some facts that the person can read and enjoy.

3. We will start this on and end it on Dec .

4. On Dec 20th, we will meet together and the Secret Santa comes forward and announces the person's name for whom they played Santa and will give him/her a gift.

5. The gift given by Secret Santa should not exceed a value of Rs.500 and preferably not lower than Rs.400. This is to ensure that it does not become a burden and at the same time both giver and receiver shouldn't feel they gave a good gift but didn't receive similarly.
